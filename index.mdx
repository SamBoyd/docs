## What is OpenBacklog?

OpenBacklog is a self-hosted project management tool designed for solo developers who want structure without the overhead of enterprise tools.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Task & Initiative Management" icon="list-check">
    Organize work into initiatives with tasks, checklists, and status tracking.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Strategic Planning" icon="compass">
    Define your Vision, Strategic Pillars, Outcomes, and Roadmap Themes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Narrative-Driven Development" icon="book-open">
    Frame your work as a story with Heroes (users), Villains (problems), and Conflicts to resolve.
  </Card>
  <Card title="MCP Integration" icon="plug">
    Bring your own AI via Claude Code, Cursor, or any MCP-compatible tool.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
**No built-in AI, no subscriptions.** OpenBacklog works with your existing AI subscriptions via the Model Context Protocol (MCP). You control your data.
</Note>
