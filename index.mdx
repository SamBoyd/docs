## What is OpenBacklog?

OpenBacklog is an AI product manager for solo developers—the strategic layer that helps you decide *what* to build, not just execute tasks.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Strategic Planning" icon="compass">
    Define your vision, pillars, outcomes, and roadmap. Think like a PM without being one.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Problem-First Planning" icon="book-open">
    Start with who you're building for and what problems they face. Your roadmap follows from there.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Task & Initiative Management" icon="list-check">
    Break strategy into initiatives and tasks. Track progress from vision to shipped code.
  </Card>
  <Card title="MCP Integration" icon="plug">
    Connect your AI (Claude Code, Cursor) via MCP. Your data, your models, no subscriptions.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
**Built for how you already work.** OpenBacklog integrates with your existing AI tools through MCP—no built-in AI to pay for, no vendor lock-in.
</Note>
